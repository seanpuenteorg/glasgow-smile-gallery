About Us

Caring for Glasgow's smiles since 1991

Glasgow Smile Gallery has been providing quality dental care and facial aesthetics to the West End community for over 30 years. Founded by Dr Andrew Bruce, our practice combines advanced techniques with a warm, patient-centred approach.

From routine check-ups to specialist dental implants and cosmetic treatments, our experienced team is committed to helping you achieve and maintain a healthy, confident smile.

Why patients choose us

  • Over 30 years of experience in Glasgow's West End
  • Specialist implant dentist on-site
  • Facial aesthetics and skin spa treatments
  • Interest-free finance available on cosmetic treatments
  • Saturday appointments available
  • NHS and private patients welcome
