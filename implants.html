Dental Implants

Specialist-led permanent tooth replacement

Dental implants are the gold standard for replacing missing teeth. Unlike dentures or bridges, implants are fixed permanently into the jawbone, providing a stable, natural-looking result that can last a lifetime with proper care.

At Glasgow Smile Gallery, our implant treatments are led by Dr Dimitrios Giatras, who holds a Master's degree in Dental Implantology from the University of Munster. With specialist training and years of experience, you can be confident you're in expert hands.

We offer a range of implant solutions including single tooth replacement, multiple tooth implants, and implant-retained dentures for patients who have lost several or all of their teeth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Most adults with good general health are suitable for dental implants. During your free consultation, Dr Giatras will assess your jawbone density, gum health and overall suitability. Even if you've been told you're not suitable elsewhere, advances in implant techniques mean more patients can benefit than ever before.
The full implant process typically takes 3 to 6 months, depending on your individual case. The implant is placed during a single appointment, then a healing period allows it to fuse with the jawbone before the final crown is attached. We'll keep you informed at every stage.
Implant placement is carried out under local anaesthetic, so you won't feel pain during the procedure. Most patients report that it's more comfortable than they expected. Mild discomfort after treatment is normal and can be managed with standard painkillers.
The cost depends on your individual treatment plan, including how many implants are needed and whether any preparatory work is required. We offer a free consultation to provide an accurate quote, and interest-free credit is available to help spread the cost.
With proper care and regular check-ups, dental implants can last a lifetime. The crown on top of the implant may need replacing after 10 to 15 years due to normal wear, but the implant itself is designed to be a permanent solution.
Yes, we offer interest-free credit on dental implant treatments. This allows you to spread the cost of your treatment into manageable monthly payments. Our team will discuss all payment options during your consultation.
