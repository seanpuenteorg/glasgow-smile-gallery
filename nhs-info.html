Understanding NHS dental care in Scotland

NHS Dental Care in Scotland

Glasgow Smile Gallery welcomes both NHS and private patients. In Scotland, NHS dental care works differently from the rest of the UK. Most adults pay 80% of the cost of their NHS dental treatment, up to a maximum charge.

If you're unsure whether you're eligible for NHS treatment or would like to register as an NHS patient, please call us on 0141 334 5166.

NHS Scotland — How It Works

  • Most adults pay 80% of treatment costs
  • Maximum patient charge applies per course of treatment
  • Children under 18 receive free NHS dental care
  • Examinations are free for all NHS patients in Scotland
  • Some adults qualify for free or reduced-cost treatment

Who Gets Free NHS Dental Care?

The following groups are entitled to free NHS dental treatment in Scotland:

Free NHS Dental Care

  • Children and young people under 26
  • Pregnant women and new mothers (up to 12 months after birth)
  • People receiving Income Support, income-based JSA, or Universal Credit
  • People receiving Pension Credit Guarantee Credit
  • People named on a valid NHS tax credit exemption certificate
  • War pensioners (for treatment of war disability)
